Assistant Professor


Department of Geology

"As a professor, my unwavering commitment lies in nurturing and supporting students in their pursuit of a rewarding career in the captivating field of geology. I believe that each student possesses unique talents and aspirations, and it is my mission to guide them on their educational journey, helping them unlock their full potential. Geology is a fascinating discipline that offers a deep understanding of our planet's history and processes. Through rigorous academic guidance, hands-on research opportunities, and mentorship, I strive to empower my students to excel in geology and to contribute meaningfully to our understanding of the Earth's dynamic environment. Together, we explore the mysteries of our planet, fostering a community of passionate geologists prepared to make a positive impact on the world."
